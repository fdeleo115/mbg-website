---
title: The Moot Board of Governors Launches
date: 2026-06-29
author: MBG
summary: Pre-law societies from across Canada come together to form a national board coordinating mooting and advocacy.
---
Today marks the launch of the **Moot Board of Governors** — a national body uniting undergraduate pre-law societies from universities across Canada.

Founded by the presidents of its member societies, the Board will coordinate national moot competitions, set shared standards, and pool advocacy resources so that students at any member school can compete and learn together.

Societies interested in joining can reach out through our [contact page](/contact/).
